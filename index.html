rent apart

This is a game about the struggles of renting in an inflated housing market, created using crowdsourced data of Trulia listings in San Francisco.

Survive by continually renting new spaces so your life force does not run out. Larger spaces (with more square footage) will give you more time to burn.

Use WASD to move around and collect money. Use this money to rent new spaces by clicking on the rental images.
